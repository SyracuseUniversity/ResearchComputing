# Azure Cloud Partnership

Cloud computing through Microsoft Azure for specific research needs.

---

## Overview

Through our continued partnership with Microsoft Azure, we can support research computing needs in the cloud. This option is available for specific scenarios where on-premises resources don't align with research requirements.

{: .warning }
**Funding Required**  
Azure cloud computing involves pay-per-use costs that will be billed to your project. You'll work with our business office to set up billing arrangements through a chartstring. Costs vary based on resources used (compute time, storage, data transfer, GPU hours, etc.).

---

## When Azure is Appropriate

{: .note }
**Cloud solutions may be appropriate when:**
- **Specific timelines:** Project deadlines that can't accommodate cluster queue times
- **Dedicated resources:** Need guaranteed access for extended periods
- **Azure-specific services:** Research requires Azure ML, specific cloud APIs, or integrations
- **Grant-funded cloud work:** Project budget specifically allocated for cloud computing
- **Scalability beyond on-prem:** Temporary need for resources beyond our infrastructure
- **Custom environments:** Need specific configurations not available on-premises

---

## What Azure Offers

### Compute Options
- **Virtual Machines:** On-demand VMs with various CPU/memory configurations
- **GPU Instances:** High-end NVIDIA GPUs available for accelerated computing
- **Custom Endpoints:** Tailored cloud services for specific needs

### Services
- **Azure ML:** Machine learning platform and model deployment services
- **Scalable Storage:** Blob storage, file shares, managed databases
- **Specialized Services:** Various Azure cloud services for research workflows

---

## Cost Structure

**Pay-per-use model:**
- Charged by usage (compute hours, storage GB, data transfer, GPU hours)
- Costs scale with continuous use
- Different resource types have different rates
- Data egress charges may apply

**Important considerations:**
- Budget planning required upfront
- Costs can accumulate quickly with 24/7 usage
- Monitor usage to stay within budget
- Stop/deallocate resources when not in use

**Cost comparison:** For most workloads, our on-premises clusters provide substantially more computational capacity at no direct charge than equivalent cloud spending would deliver. We'll help analyze whether cloud is cost-effective for your specific needs.

---

## Business Office Coordination

Using Azure requires working with Syracuse University's business office:

1. **Chartstring required:** Cloud costs billed to your project account
2. **Budget approval:** Ensure funding is available and authorized for cloud computing
3. **Setup process:** We coordinate with business office for billing configuration
4. **Ongoing monitoring:** You'll receive usage reports and cost tracking

---

## Getting Started with Azure

During your consultation, we'll review:

1. Whether cloud computing is appropriate for your research
2. Estimated costs based on your requirements
3. Funding source and budget availability
4. Business office billing setup process
5. Alternative on-premises options that may better suit your needs
6. Custom environment or endpoint requirements

{: .highlight }
**Interested in Azure Cloud Computing?**  
Email [researchcomputing@syr.edu](mailto:researchcomputing@syr.edu) with:
- Your research project and timeline
- Why you're considering cloud computing
- Your funding source, chartstring, and budget
- Computational requirements (CPU, GPU, storage, services needed)

We'll schedule a consultation to review options and help determine if cloud computing is the right fit - or if our on-premises resources can meet your needs more cost-effectively.
